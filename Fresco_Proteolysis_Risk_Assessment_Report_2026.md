## Executive Summary

This report re-evaluates the proteolysis risk status of the Caravaggio fresco fragment (Panel 3B) based on the original 2023 baseline analysis and assumed advancements in conservation science from 2024. The original assessment classified this fragment as **HIGH RISK** with predicted 15-20% binder integrity loss within 5 years if untreated.

Recent hypothetical developments from the Getty Conservation Institute suggest two significant breakthroughs:
1. **Nanoparticle-stabilized enzyme inhibitors** enabling targeted in-situ treatment of protein binders
2. **Sub-visual proteolysis detection** through advanced environmental monitoring

This assessment integrates these assumed advancements with the 2023 baseline to propose a revised mitigation strategy with improved prognosis.
